**University of Florida Online** (UF Online) is one of the most visible fully online bachelor’s options in the U.S. for 2026—especially for **transfer students**, **working adults**, and **veterans** who want a **public research university** credential without relocating to Gainesville. This guide summarizes how UF Online works, what it costs, how transfer credit fits, and how to decide if it matches your goals—using **official UF sources** and recognized rankings where cited.

**Quick facts:** UF Online reports **No. 1** nationally for **Best Online Bachelor’s Programs**, **Best Online Bachelor’s Programs for Veterans**, and **Best Online Business Programs** in *U.S. News & World Report*’s 2026 online rankings, and publishes per-credit tuition for **2025–2026** on its site. Always confirm **major availability**, **prerequisites**, and **tuition** on [UF Online](https://ufonline.ufl.edu) before you apply.

## Key takeaways

- **Same degree**: UF Online is the University of Florida’s fully online undergraduate pathway; UF states that online students earn the **same accredited UF degree** as on-campus students, with courses taught by **UF faculty** ([UF Online](https://ufonline.ufl.edu), [UF Online & distance learning overview](https://www.ufl.edu/academics/online-distance-learning/)).
- **Scale of offerings**: UF Online advertises **24 fully online undergraduate majors** across multiple colleges ([UF Online degrees](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/degrees/undergraduate/)).
- **Rankings**: UF highlights **#1** placements in multiple *U.S. News* 2026 online categories ([UF Online news: 2026 rankings](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/news-and-updates/2026-usnwr-rankings/)).
- **Transfers**: UF Online is built around **lower-division** and **upper-division** transfer paths; Florida **A.A.** graduates can receive **60 credits** when requirements are met ([UF Online transfer credit](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/transfer-credit/)).
- **Tuition (2025–2026)**: UF Online lists **$129.18** per credit (Florida resident) and **$552.62** per credit (non-resident), tuition plus required fees ([UF Online tuition & fees](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/tuition/tuition-fees/)).

## What is UF Online?

UF Online is UF’s **fully online bachelor’s** program for students who complete their degree **without relocating** to campus. UF emphasizes that **online courses are taught by the same faculty** who teach on campus, and that students earn the **same UF degree** ([UF Online](https://ufonline.ufl.edu); [UF academics: online & distance learning](https://www.ufl.edu/academics/online-distance-learning/)).

It is designed for students who need **flexibility**—including **working professionals**, **parents**, **military-affiliated students**, and **community college transfers**—while still accessing UF’s **student support** ecosystem ([UF Online student success](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/current-students/student-success/)).

## Why UF Online stands out in 2026

### National rankings and consistency

UF Online promotes strong *U.S. News* outcomes for 2026, including **#1 Best Online Bachelor’s Programs** overall, **#1 for Veterans**, and **#1 Online Bachelor’s in Business** ([UF Online: 2026 rankings](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/news-and-updates/2026-usnwr-rankings/)). UF also notes a long run of **top-five** national placement for its online bachelor’s offering ([same source](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/news-and-updates/2026-usnwr-rankings/)).

### Affordability positioning

UF Online frequently cites **lower cost than residential attendance** and states that online students save about **40%** compared to residential tuition, with Florida residents paying **75%** of standard in-state tuition ([UF Online tuition & fees](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/tuition/tuition-fees/)). For a third-party snapshot of UF Online’s positioning among online colleges, see [Newsweek’s 2026 online college rankings](https://rankings.newsweek.com/americas-top-online-colleges-2026)—but treat **official UF tuition pages** as authoritative for pricing.

### Program breadth

UF Online advertises **24 online majors** spanning areas such as **business**, **health-related fields**, **communication**, **education**, **STEM**, and more ([UF Online undergraduate degrees](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/degrees/undergraduate/)). Major availability can be **competitive** or **prerequisite-driven**, so treat the degree list as a starting point, not a guarantee of admission to a specific major.

## Admissions: lower-division vs upper-division transfers

UF Online admissions are structured around **transfer credit levels**. Two common entry paths are:

- **Lower-division transfer**: typically **12–59** transferable credits ([UF Online: lower-division transfers](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/admissions-requirements/lower-division-transfers/)).
- **Upper-division transfer**: typically **60+** credits or an **A.A. degree** (where applicable) ([UF Online: upper-division transfers](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/admissions-requirements/upper-division-transfers/)).

### GPA requirements (baseline)

UF Online publishes baseline expectations such as a **minimum 2.0 cumulative GPA** and performance expectations for the **last institution attended**—with the caveat that **competitive majors** may require **higher GPAs** and **specific coursework** ([UF Online: upper-division transfers](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/admissions-requirements/upper-division-transfers/)).

If you are applying to a **limited-access** or **high-demand** program, assume you’ll need **more than the minimum** and plan prerequisites early.

## Transfer credits and the Florida A.A.

UF Online emphasizes a **transfer-friendly** model:

- Students who earn a **Florida A.A.** from a **public** community/state college may receive **60 credits**, the maximum typically transferred from a two-year school ([UF Online transfer credit](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/transfer-credit/)).
- UF states that **credits do not expire** and are evaluated for **course equivalency**; coursework that doesn’t equate may still count as **elective** credit when allowed ([UF Online transfer credit](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/transfer-credit/)).

If you’re close to an **upper-division** pathway, review UF’s pages for **upper-division** requirements and any **major-specific** prerequisites ([upper-division transfers](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/admissions-requirements/upper-division-transfers/)).

## Who UF Online fits best

UF Online tends to work well for students who:

- Want a **well-known public university** brand and a **fully online** bachelor’s pathway ([UF Online](https://ufonline.ufl.edu))
- Plan to enter as a **transfer** with **some college credit** already earned ([transfer requirements](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/admissions-requirements/upper-division-transfers/))
- Need **schedule flexibility** while working or supporting family ([UF Online](https://ufonline.ufl.edu))
- Value a structured support model (**OneStop**, advising, tutoring) ([OneStop](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/current-students/uf-online-onestop/))

It may be a weaker fit if you need a **major UF doesn’t offer online**, want **in-person labs** without a hybrid plan, or can’t meet **major-specific** prerequisites for competitive programs.

## FAQ

### Is UF Online the same as the University of Florida?

Yes—UF Online is UF’s fully online bachelor’s pathway, and UF markets the same **accredited UF degree** and **faculty-led** instruction ([UF Online](https://ufonline.ufl.edu)).

### How many majors does UF Online offer?

UF Online lists **24** fully online undergraduate majors ([UF distance learning overview](https://www.ufl.edu/academics/online-distance-learning/); [UF Online degrees](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/degrees/undergraduate/)).

### Is UF Online good for transfer students?

Generally, yes—especially for students with **60+** credits or a completed **Florida A.A.**, subject to evaluation rules ([lower-division transfers](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/admissions-requirements/lower-division-transfers/); [upper-division transfers](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/admissions-requirements/upper-division-transfers/); [transfer credit](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/transfer-credit/)).

### What is UF Online tuition per credit?

For **2025–2026**, UF Online lists **$129.18** per credit for **Florida residents** and **$552.62** for **non-residents** (tuition plus required fees) ([UF Online tuition & fees](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/tuition/tuition-fees/)).

### Do UF Online credits expire?

UF states transfer **credits do not expire** and are evaluated for equivalency ([UF Online transfer credit](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/transfer-credit/)).

### What GPA do you need?

UF publishes baseline minimums (commonly **2.0** cumulative in referenced materials), but **competitive majors** may require more ([upper-division transfers](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/admissions-requirements/upper-division-transfers/)).

### Is financial aid available?

Yes—UF Online notes **federal**, **state**, and **institutional** aid may apply ([UF Online tuition & fees](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/tuition/tuition-fees/)).

## People also ask

### Is UF Online accredited?

Yes—UF Online degrees are awarded through the **accredited University of Florida** ([UF Online](https://ufonline.ufl.edu)).

### Does UF Online offer graduate degrees?

UF Online’s undergraduate-focused marketing emphasizes **bachelor’s** completion; other UF units may offer separate online graduate programs ([UF distance learning overview](https://www.ufl.edu/academics/online-distance-learning/)).

### Can you finish faster with transfer credit?

Many students can accelerate completion after upper-division transfer, depending on major requirements ([upper-division transfers](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/admissions-requirements/upper-division-transfers/)).

### Is UF Online “hard” to get into?

Selectivity varies—**minimum GPAs** are not the same as **competitive** admission for high-demand majors ([upper-division transfers](https://ufonline.ufl.edu/apply/admissions-requirements/upper-division-transfers/)).
